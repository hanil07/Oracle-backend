Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Entrovibe Launchpad ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses and protects information when you visit this website or purchase our digital product.

1. Information we collect

When you purchase or interact with Entrovibe Launchpad, we may collect:

  • Your email address (for delivery and support).
  • Payment information, processed securely via Superprofile (India) and/or Gumroad (Global) — we never store your card details.
  • Basic usage data via the Meta Pixel (page views and on-site events).

2. How we use your information

  • To deliver your purchase and provide access.
  • To send support communications.
  • To improve our advertising via the Meta Pixel (anonymized).

3. Meta Pixel

This site uses the Meta Pixel to track conversions and optimize ad campaigns. This data is anonymized and used only for advertising performance. You can opt out via Facebook's privacy settings.

4. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Payment processing is handled by Superprofile (India) and Gumroad (Global) — please refer to their respective privacy policies.

5. Data retention

We retain your email address for support and communication purposes. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us.
